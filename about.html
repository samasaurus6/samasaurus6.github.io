About Us

Using Minecraft as a Medium


Polymaps is a small team of talented individuals with a passion for Minecraft and other video games.

As a founding partner of the official Minecraft Marketplace, Polymaps works directly with Microsoft and Mojang to provide players with fun and unique experiences.

With over 7 years of working together, the team have experience in 3D modelling, graphic design & texturing, sound design, video editing, programming and Unity game development.
